The GCU Technology Club is the official club of College of Technology.
Our mission is to celebrate and promote interest and innovation in every
field of technology. Our goal is to cultivate an environment for like-minded
students to pursue their interests and share their passions by engaging with
others in projects, learning, and community. We work with different organizations
such as Black Hat, DEFCON, Girls Who Code, ISACA, Cactus Con and many more.
We aim to prepare students for highly successful careers in the technology industry
by supplying opportunities to gain industry connections and leadership experience.
We encourage technological innovation and work to expose our members to diverse
disciplines. Additionally, we strive to use our knowledge to provide excellent
